However, the app may automatically collect limited technical data through third-party services used to operate the app, such as:

• Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)

Collected data may be used for:

• Preventing fraud or abuse

Advertising

This app displays ads provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may use advertising identifiers and device information to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

You can reset or disable your device’s advertising ID anytime from your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services that collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. These services help us operate the app, analyze usage, and serve advertisements.

Payments

Any purchases are processed securely through the app store provider. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect app data and ensure secure operation. Since chat files are processed locally, they are not transmitted to our servers.

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
